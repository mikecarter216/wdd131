Nigeria

Nigeria is a vibrant country with a rich cultural heritage and diverse landscapes. It is the most populous country in Africa and home to over 200 million people, representing hundreds of ethnic groups, including the Yoruba, Igbo, Akwa Ibom and Hausa. Some of the most fascinating aspects of Nigeria include its thriving film industry, Nollywood, which is the second-largest in the world, and the famous Nigerian cuisine, known for its rich flavors and variety. Nigeria is also renowned for its natural beauty, from the stunning Zuma Rock to the breathtaking Obudu Mountain Resort. The country’s music scene has taken the world by storm, with Afrobeats dominating international charts. Nigeria's diverse culture, history, and innovation make it one of the most exciting countries to learn about and explore.
